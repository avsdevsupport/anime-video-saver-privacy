Privacy Policy

Anime Video Saver — Effective date: April 9, 2026

Anime Video Saver is a Chrome extension that helps users detect downloadable video links on supported episode pages and download the current episode when a supported stream or direct media file is available.

What the extension does

Anime Video Saver reads the current supported page and observes related media requests in order to identify downloadable video resources such as HLS streams or direct MP4 files for the current episode page.

Data the extension may process

When the user uses the extension, Anime Video Saver may process:

  • The URL of the current tab
  • The title of the current page
  • Media request URLs needed to detect downloadable video resources
  • Basic local extension settings, such as the user’s preferred quality selection

What the extension does not collect

Anime Video Saver does not intentionally collect or store:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Payment information
  • Passwords or login credentials
  • Personal messages
  • Health information

How data is used

The extension uses the above information only for its single purpose:

  • Detecting downloadable video resources on the current supported page
  • Showing available download options
  • Allowing the user to download the current episode
  • Saving basic local settings such as preferred quality

Data sharing

Anime Video Saver does not sell or transfer user data to third parties for advertising, profiling, or unrelated purposes.

The extension does not use collected data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

Data storage

Basic settings such as preferred quality may be stored locally in the user’s browser using Chrome extension storage.

Anime Video Saver does not maintain a remote user database for this functionality.

Supported sites and limitations

Anime Video Saver does not support every website.

Some websites may use DRM-protected playback or delivery methods that do not expose downloadable video links to the browser extension. In such cases, the extension may not be able to detect direct download options.

Security

Anime Video Saver is designed to operate locally in the user’s browser for its stated purpose.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
